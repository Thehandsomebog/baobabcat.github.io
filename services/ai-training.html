AI Training & Enablement

Equip your team with the skills and confidence to leverage AI effectively -- from prompt engineering to hands-on tool proficiency, we make AI accessible for everyone in your organization.

What It Is & Why It Matters

AI is only as powerful as the people using it. Without proper training, even the most advanced AI tools go underutilized or, worse, produce unreliable results. BaobabCat's AI Training & Enablement program bridges the gap between cutting-edge technology and your team's day-to-day workflows, ensuring every employee can harness AI with confidence.

Our training goes far beyond surface-level demos. We teach prompt engineering fundamentals, show your team how to evaluate AI outputs critically, and build lasting habits that integrate AI into real business processes. Whether your team is brand-new to AI or looking to sharpen advanced skills, we design programs that meet them where they are.

The result is an AI-literate workforce that drives innovation from within -- reducing dependence on external consultants and unlocking the full value of your AI investments.

Prompt Engineering Workshops

Master the art of communicating with AI. Learn how to craft prompts that produce accurate, useful, and consistent outputs across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other leading models.

AI Tool Proficiency Training

Executive AI Briefings

Ongoing Support & Coaching

How It Works

Curriculum Design

Based on the assessment, we build a custom training program aligned with your industry, workflows, and business goals -- no generic slide decks or one-size-fits-all content.

Interactive Training

We deliver engaging, hands-on sessions where your team works with real AI tools on real business scenarios. Every participant leaves with practical skills they can apply immediately.

Follow-up & Support

After the initial training, we provide ongoing coaching, refresher sessions, and access to updated resources as AI tools evolve -- keeping your team ahead of the curve.

FAQ

How long does a typical training program take?

Most programs run between 1 and 4 weeks, depending on team size and depth of coverage. A focused prompt engineering workshop can be completed in a single day, while a comprehensive enablement program with multiple modules and follow-up coaching typically spans 2-4 weeks. We design the timeline around your schedule and learning goals.

Do participants need any prior AI experience?

Not at all. Our programs are designed to meet your team at their current skill level. We start with a skills assessment so we can create appropriate learning tracks -- from complete beginners who have never used an AI tool to power users looking to refine advanced techniques like chain-of-thought prompting and multi-step workflows.

Can the training be delivered remotely?

Yes. We offer both in-person and virtual training formats. Our virtual sessions use interactive tools, breakout rooms, and live exercises to ensure the same level of engagement and hands-on learning as our in-person workshops. We also offer hybrid options for distributed teams.
