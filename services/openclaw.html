What Is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open-source AI assistant platform that lets you deploy intelligent chatbots and virtual assistants across every channel your business uses. Unlike closed-source alternatives that lock you into a single vendor, OpenClaw gives you complete control over your data, your integrations, and your customer experience.

With OpenClaw, your AI assistant can operate simultaneously on WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, web chat, and SMS, providing consistent, accurate responses trained on your own knowledge base. It connects to over 50 services out of the box, from CRMs and helpdesks to scheduling tools and payment processors, so your assistant can do more than just answer questions.

Most importantly, OpenClaw offers full data sovereignty. Your business data stays yours, whether you deploy on-premises or in your own cloud environment. No third-party vendor has access to your conversations, customer data, or proprietary knowledge.

FAQ

What is OpenClaw?
OpenClaw is an open-source AI assistant platform that allows businesses to deploy intelligent chatbots across multiple communication channels. It provides a unified interface for building, training, and managing AI assistants with full control over your data and integrations.
Which platforms does OpenClaw support?
OpenClaw supports deployment across WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, web chat widgets, and SMS. New platform integrations are added regularly, and custom channel integrations can be built to fit your specific needs.
How long does it take to deploy an OpenClaw assistant?
A typical OpenClaw deployment takes 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the complexity of your use case, the number of platform integrations, and the size of your knowledge base. Simple single-channel deployments can be completed in as little as one week.
How does OpenClaw handle data privacy?
OpenClaw gives you full data sovereignty. You can deploy it on your own servers or in your private cloud environment, meaning your customer conversations and business data never leave your infrastructure. No third-party vendor has access to your data.
