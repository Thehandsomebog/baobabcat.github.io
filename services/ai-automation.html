AI Workflow Automation

Clear offer: automate repeatable operational work across intake, triage, document handling, and internal handoffs without making the workflow harder to supervise.

Who it is for

Teams buried in repetitive work, queue management, and manual coordination across functions.

Business outcomes

Time recovered, cleaner handoffs, reduced queue friction, and fewer expensive humans doing low-judgment tasks.

Typical automation targets

  • email or support triage
  • document extraction and routing
  • lead qualification and intake
  • status updates and internal handoffs

What makes it work

The workflow has to be redesigned around the automation. BaobabCat builds the decision path, exception handling, and human review layer so the automation does not become a new source of noise.
